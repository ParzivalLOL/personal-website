AI Finger-Pointer

The AI Finger-pointer allows users to transfer the whole functionality of their mouse over to their fingers. You can click, scroll and type without being in physical contact with the computer. Having viewed many surgeries during my internship at Artemis Hospital, I understand the importance of having sterile conditions. This would allow surgeons and nurses to operate computers without touching anything. Watch the video on the right to find out more!

Moth Classifer - AI/ML

The Moth Classifier allows users to input any photo of a moth and the website will return the most and second-most likely species along with the respective confidences. The program uses Python and YOLOv8 image classification model for machine learnig. I trained and tested the model on a publicly available dataset.
